About Argho & Samrat

Argho and Samrat are legendary local explorers who believe the best journeys happen close to home. Travelling by bike from Jagrihi to 22 Bigha and Khariberia, they enjoy village roads, quiet turns, and food breaks — especially ghugni.

They love village nature, birds, village houses, peaceful atmosphere, and watching sunsets under open skies.
